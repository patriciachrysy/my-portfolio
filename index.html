Who am I?

A young girl who first got in touch a computer at 10 years and who felt in love with programing at 17 while studying the subject in university. I started to professionally work in the field 5 years ago, starting a very interesting intership a Dev'Op (the big data field). I later decided to take another direction with web and mobile hybrid mobile app development which later came up to be my "cup of tea". I started with backend with Php and of its best frameworks out there, Symfony. I almost all my projects with it( about 10) including webapp and websites. I also created mobile apps with Ionic Framework for android, ios and windows phone platform.

After occupying the position of junior backend and mobile developper in 2 different startups for 4 years, i finally choosed to take a break to join a bootcamp to expand my knowledge to frontend and also get to learn another backend language, which i did by joigning Microverse in 2020 for 8 months. I got to learn MicroverseRuby and its framework Ruby On Rails, but also couple it with React/Redux and Javascript. I ended up earning a certificate as MicroverseFull stack developer and some amazing professionalism skills. Now i am ready to get back to the battlefield ;-)
